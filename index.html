Welcome to the Medic Pharmacy App

About the App

The Medic Pharmacy App is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline pharmacy operations, manage inventory, track prescriptions, and enhance patient care. This platform aims to provide an efficient and user-friendly experience for both pharmacists and customers.

Key Features

  • Inventory Management: Keep track of all medications, supplies, and stock levels.
  • Prescription Handling: Efficiently manage new and refill prescriptions.
  • User Management: Secure roles for administrators, pharmacists, and customers.
  • Reporting & Analytics: Generate reports on sales, inventory, and patient data.
  • Secure & Reliable: Built with security best practices to protect sensitive data.
